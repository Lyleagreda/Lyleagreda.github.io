LESSON 1

Introduction to Computing typically covers fundamental concepts in computer science and information technology. Us students learn about the basics of computer systems. This lesson may also introduce key topics like computer hardware, software, networking, and the impact of computing on society. Students often gain foundational skills in problem-solving and logical thinking, setting the groundwork for more advanced studies in computing.

LESSON 2

The Professions & careers in computing offers diverse opportunities, including roles such as software developer, network engineer, data scientist, cybersecurity analyst, systems analyst, database administrator, and IT project manager.

LESSON 3 & 4

The information processing cycle is a sequence of steps that computers follow to process data, including input, processing, output, and storage. Computer systems encompass hardware and software components that work together to perform tasks and execute the information processing cycle.

LESSON 5

The basic concept of computer security involves safeguarding computer systems and data from unauthorized access, damage, or theft, ensuring confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.
